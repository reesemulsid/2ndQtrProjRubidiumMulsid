Herrscher of Sentience

Fu Hua was born around 50,000 years ago during the Previous Era. She grew up in the slums, and was raised by her father who owned and ran a dojo. Just as she began settling in her new school, a Honkai Impact ravaged her birthplace, the city of Sapphire. She fled the city, barely surviving until she was rescued by MOTH’s Squad V leader, HIMEKO.

Fu Hua used to be a member of the Fire Moth organization about 50000 years ago in the last civilization. She is one of the few people who survived against the Final Herrscher. Later she became the guardian of Shenzhou, coming to be known as The Sage and devoting her life to eliminating the Houkai.


Otto shot Fu Hua right in the head in the Battle of Schicksal. With no better choice, Fu Hua transferred her sentience to a Fenghuang Down's feather, leaving her body empty. This body of a MANTIS was healthy, strong, and highly adaptable to Honkai energy. The will of Honkai naturally wanted to take it for itself, so it injected a new sentience into her body, which was the Herrscher of Sentience. But the will of Honkai didn't know that tens of thousands of years of memories had been stored in her brain. The sea of memories deluged the mind of the newborn Herrscher persona, making the Herrscher of Sentience mistake herself for Fu Hua when she awakened.

It wasn’t until after the two went all over Mount Taixuan, collecting other feathers to help HoS restore “her” memories, did HoS begin to doubt herself and Fu Hua’s existence. Though she didn’t want to absorb Fu Hua’s feather, since she had the other as a companion for long, she also felt that Fu Hua was challenging her identity by claiming to be the real Fu Hua. After being prompted by the Will, HoS approaches Fu Hua and explains her inner dilemma. Fu Hua only warns her that she cannot absorb her, and after an attempt, HoS realizes the other is right, leaving her even more confused. To settle their debate on who was the “real Fu Hua” however, HoS spots the Hyperion ship that has just arrived in the mountains and gleefully remarks that they now have two judges to help finally settle her identity crisis.
