Herrscher of Reason

Bronya Zaychik was born in Siberia, shortly before the second Honkai incident. The Honkai attack in 2000 caused Siberia and surrounding regions to fall into disorder and many children were placed into the Army, where they were trained to become cold-blooded killers. Bronya was one such orphan, and became a skilled sniper earning the nickname, Ural Silver Wolf.

Bronya was sent to Russia to assassinate a high-priority target, Cocolia. Bronya failed, and was instead captured by her target, who brought her to her own orphanage. Unknown to Bronya and the other children, the orphanage was part of Anti-Entropy and they were injected with the Honkai virus for Experiment X-10.


The Herrscher of Reason is the latest battlesuit of Bronya Zaychik as of 2021. The battlesuit ties into the new chapter of the main story because it deals with the Eye of the Deep. The location is the location of the Gem of Reason.

Bronya dives into the Eye to save her beloved Seele Vollerei as she became trapped in the Quantum Sea after undergoing the X-10 experiment as its 1st test subject. To save Seele, Bronya taps into the power of the core and manifests it into a battlesuit which embodies Bronya's vision of 'Ideal'. The power of Herrscher of Reason is the ability to construct things (human creations) as long as they know how it works. Bronya's use of this ability mostly revolves around the Remodelled Bunny. She was not able to construct huge armors, warship or fighters like Welt, but she can reconstruct 19C and its weapons.

Although Bronya gained resistance against Houkai through the X-10 experiment, owning a Herrscher core is still a dangerous risk. If she uses Herrscher power for a prolonged amount of time, her body will become corrupted, similar to what happened with Welt and Himeko. But Bronya has no time to think about the future as her greatest desire is to find the gems in the Quantum Sea. To dive into the depths to find her beloved childhood friend Seele, and possibly save Welt as well.

Herrscher of Thunder

Raiden Mei absorbed all residual Honkai energy in Nagazora and awakened by her own will as the Herrscher of Thunder.

Herrscher of Sentience

The Herrscher of Sentience formed in Fu Hua but instead of becoming instantly destructive like a regular Herrscher, Fu Hua's memories made it want to fight the Honkai.
